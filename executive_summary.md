## Main Findings

- West is the strongest region by revenue with $198,702.
- Electronics leads category revenue at $334,416.
- Stationery has the healthiest profit margin at 55.5%.
- Online has the lowest channel margin at 27.4%, so discounting and fulfillment costs should be reviewed.
- Seasonal demand is visible in the monthly trend, with late-year months outperforming early-year months.

## Recommended Actions

1. Increase inventory and campaign budget for Electronics in the best-performing regions.
2. Review discount policies in the Online channel to protect margin.
3. Use the high-margin category mix as the default recommendation set for online campaigns.
4. Monitor customer satisfaction alongside promotions so revenue growth does not come from over-discounting.
